We are a group of entusiastic guides who want to provide you with an unforgettable experience of seeing Kyiv's unique historical places and hearing their stories. Our tour will take you from Khreschatyk where our office is based to a beautiful Dnipro river on Obolon.

Route

  • Khreschatyk (Khymera Building, Maidan Nezalezhnosti)
  • Zoloti Vorota (Golden Gates)
  • Sophiivskiy Sobor (Sophiivska Cathedral and Volodymirskiy Park)
  • Andriivska Tserkva(Andriivska Chruch and Andriivskiy Uzviz)
  • Podil (Museums and monuments)
  • Tarasa Shevchenka Street
  • Petrivka
  • Obolon
  • Natalka Park, Dnipro river
